ABOUT US

Juice events was born out of a desire to create a truly client focussed agency with a strong creative twist.
The business was founded in 2013 following our dream to have a small, bespoke yet passionate and experienced team.
The team were picked because of their obsession with the industry and their excellent experience and dedication to events.

Our Offerings

We tell the story of your business, brand or ethos through events. We start by sitting down with you to understand the objective of your event and we then help you achieve this by providing creative, exciting and ‘spot on’ solutions.
We love that no two days are the same – one day we will run a conference, the next a fully themed Christmas party. We like the variety and it makes for an experienced team. So, if you’re looking for an inspirational charity dinner, stand out awards dinner, or memorable graduation ball or any other event, please do give us a call and look at our case studies for inspiration.
Over our first five years, we have run the widest variety of memorable events, from product launches and Christmas parties to treasure hunts and exhibitions – and we love that every event is totally different. Whatever the event, our ethos is always the same – we design for impact but we love detail, as this is what everyone remembers.
